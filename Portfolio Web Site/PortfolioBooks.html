Excerpt

“Hoooooowl!” The sound was getting closer as I ran through the forest. I had seen these woods before and they were very pretty, especially at night, but I wasn’t sightseeing this time. As I ran, my only thought was, ‘I have to get away; I have to get away.’ I ended up so lost in my thoughts that by the time I saw the slope, it was too late. I tumbled down, hitting tree after tree, rock after rock. It felt like being beaten with a bat. After what seemed like an hour, but was probably only a couple of minutes, I came to a halt. My body hurt like the dickens and I just laid there, but then I remembered why I was running. I got up slowly and painfully, about to start running again, but it was too late. It was here.
I looked into the deep yellow eyes of what would be my killer. I saw its long claws digging into the wet earth, its fangs dripping with the flesh of its last kill, and I had never felt so afraid. At the same time though, I felt this pride that I was actually looking at it, the biggest wolf known-well unknown-to humankind. Before I could revel in that accomplishment for long, it lunged and…
I woke up with a start; boy did I hate that dream. I’ve been having it for the past few weeks and every time it happened, I would wake up in a cold sweat. I looked at my clock and saw that it was 4:30. No sense in going back to sleep. I probably couldn’t anyway so I got up to go take a shower. The warm water pounding my face and body helped calm my nerves and then since it was still early, school not starting for another 2 hours, I decided to go make my family breakfast.

Characters

Jake : The protagonist who is 15 years old is the older brother to Lex who is 2 years younger. He is skeptical of the supernatural world, but after being bitten by a werewolf he becomes a believer. As a human he is athletic and is a talented soccer player, but as a werewolf, his senses and movements become further enhanced. He is kind-hearted, but can hold a grudge.

Alexander “Lex” : Jake’s younger brother who is very intelligent, but also a lover and believer of most supernatural things. He is more quiet than his brother and trusts people less easily, but is always ready to help somebody when they need it.

Nolan : Jake’s best friend who is always around him. Has ADHD which causes him to get distracted easily, but when you need him to, he can focus on one thing even when there are distractions. He has green eyes and red spiky hair which contrasts his army-like attire of camo shirts with black jeans, suspenders, and combat boots.

Book Vocabulary

Weresiber : A werewolf, but instead of the person turing into part wolf, they turn into part siberian tiger.
